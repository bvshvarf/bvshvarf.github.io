Imagine a world where researchers can process complex biological data effortlessly. At Group Green, we’re revolutionizing high-throughput sequencing by providing an intuitive web portal known as DataSeq, that automates data preprocessing for RNA-, ATAC-, and WGBS-Sequencing.

With a user-friendly interface, our platform eliminates the need for advanced technical skills, allowing scientists to focus on what truly matters: analyzing data and driving discoveries. No more cumbersome scripts or complex setups—just simple checkbox selections to handle large datasets quickly and efficiently.

As the demand for streamlined preprocessing tools grows, DataSeq is created to empower researchers globally, enhance collaboration, and accelerate breakthroughs in biological research. Join us in transforming how scientists work with sequencing data!
